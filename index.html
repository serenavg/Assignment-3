What's Behind the Green Glass Door?

This is a riddle game. You will type your guesses into the box. If you get three correct answers in a row, you win the game!

Your challenge is to figure out what belongs behind the Green Glass Door. Most things won't belong. You will type ONE word into the box, then press ENTER. An X will come up if you are wrong, and a correct banner will come up if you are right.

Good luck on solving this riddle game.

Hint: forks and cars can't be behind the door, but trees can!

ps, if you know how it works, don't spoil it for anyone else.
